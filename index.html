About Me



Hi! I'm Sotiris Chatoupis but you can call me Sot!

I studied electrical engineering but later on,I found myself falling in love with design and especially product design.

I am interested in all stages of Design Thinking, while I love working closely with all other fields such as marketing, business, dev teams and pms.

My NN

As part of PWC team, I redesigned the UI/UX of myNN portal for NN Hellas, both for web and mobile app. This was a nice challenge for two main reasons. I had to deliver a fresh look while keeping in mind the very strict brand/design quidelines of the NN group. wappier

I worked for over 5 years in wappier and I had the chance to work on many different projects and gain a lot of experience as a product designer. Wappier provides through its platform the tools to maximize user life time value in mobile apps & games.

Herocam

Herocam is an app based on image recognition which aims to make the tourist experience better. I designed the UI nad UX of the app. The aim was to be fresh, easy adn fast to use. It's available on the App Store.

TapOnIt Deals

TapOnIt Deals is a mobile site that drives traffic and sales to local businesses while making it easy for people to save money and try new things. Users get deals through sms and then they are redirected to the taponit mobile site where they can see more deals, redeem the ones they want and collect loyalty points to get amazing rewards.

One of my projects in wappier was to redesign the taponit mobile deals web site, make it more functional and improve the experience of the user. We are still working with taponit on daily basis to improve the TapOnIt deals experience!

Wizzo

Wizzo is an app that rewards the game lovers! More than 5 million players use Wizzo to upload and stream videos, win prizes and experience new games.

I have to say this was one of my favorite projects when we were a small team in the early years of wappier. We basically managed just a handful of talented people to build a top quality product. I led the design of the UI/UX of the app. One intersting aspect of this app is that is targeted for users of the MENA region so had to design all screens and flows on RTL as well but also having in mind the different culture of the target users.

Beautly

Beautly is my attempt in entrepreneurship along with other 3 good friends and talented people. We basically created a service that connects women that needs beauty services in the comfort and flexibility of their home with beauty professionals of all kinds. So we basically created two apps, one for the customers and one for the professionals. We run this as a side project, we put it out there on the world and it was really fun to see people actually using it! But from a business perspective we didnt manage to make it profitable enough. Lot's of lessons learnt for the next attempt :) I was the designer of the team so I designed everything from the two apps, the logo, the website to the ads.

Augomaxies

On covid time many families couldn't be together so we decide with a good friend (and ninja developer), Kyriakos Leivadas, to create an app with the same experience, where pepople could hit eggs without being in the same place!

This is a really cheesy and fun app and was trending on the easter holidays! We went from the idea to a working app in less tha five days, and this was after worκ hours. That's a personal record for me :P
