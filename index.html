About

My name is Sotiris Chatoupis and I'm a passionate self-taught designer with a background in electrical engineering. I believe in combining imagination and logic to create useful products that make people's life better.

I am intersted in all stages of design, from the idea to the final product. Invision, Photoshop and Illustrator are my favorite toys. Github, HTML, CSS and JS are some new friends that help me widen my creative horizons.
