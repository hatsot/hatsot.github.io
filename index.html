My name is Sotos1 Chatoupis and I'm a passionate self-taught designer with a background in electrical engineering.

TapOnIt Deals

TapOnIt Deals is a mobile site that drives traffic and sales to local businesses while making it easy for people to save money and try new things. Users get deals through sms and then they are redirected to the taponit mobile site where they can see more deals, redeem the ones they want and collect loyalty points to get amazing rewards.

One of my projects in wappier was to redesign the taponit mobile deals web site, make it more functional and improve the experience of the user. We are still working with taponit on daily basis to improve the TapOnIt deals experience!
